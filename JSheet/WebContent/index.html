Malla Reddy Engineering College(Autonomous)

Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC) was started in the year 2002 under the Chairmanship of Sri.Ch. Malla Reddy, M.P, who has vast experience in providing high quality technical education, on par with premier Institutions like NITs and IITs in the country. MREC was started with the approval of AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to JNTUH, Hyderabad. The college is situated in a serene, lush green environment on Kompally- Bahadhurpally Road, opposite to Forest academy, R.R.District, Telangana State.
